Courses

A diversity of sectors in the Tertiary and Technological fields

Computing-Telecommunications-Electronics-Networks (ITER)

Training offers
  • IT
  • Computing - Microelectronics
  • Computing - Electrical Engineering - Automation
Training objectives

The objectives of the training can be summarized as follows:

  • Locally create a center of excellence in training and research and development in the field of networks, telecommunications, computing and multimedia;
  • Train professionals for the new professions of architects, integrators and developers of communicating services;
  • Develop training, skills and research on new uses of ICTs;
  • Train high-level, multilingual executives, combining solid technical skills, managerial qualities, practices of non-European economic systems and likely to experience high-level "positive" international mobility.

Geology-Mine-Oil-Environment (GMPE)

Training offers
  • Earth and mineral resource sciences
  • Water and environmental management
Training objectives

The objectives of the training can be summarized as follows:

  • The training of the GMPE department aims to provide Côte d'Ivoire and Africa with high-level senior managers capable of mastering the following professional activity sectors respectively:
    • Mining Engineering and Metallogenies
    • Petroleum and Refining Engineering
    • Geohazards and Environmental Engineering
    • Sanitation and Sanitary Engineering

Information, Communication - Human Resources (IC-RH)

Training offers
  • Journalism - Production / Multimedia
  • Marketing - Advertising
  • Human resources management and organization management
Training objectives

The objectives of the training can be summarized as follows:

  • Offer a multidisciplinary approach to communication and media articulating theoretical knowledge, analysis and practical applications
  • Combine the "general to the particular" diagram and the theoretical frameworks with a strong approach towards scenarios, achievements, field studies, etc.
  • Promote diversified recruitment and offer guidance towards several masters.

Agrifood Industry (IAA)

Training offers
  • Engineering of bio-productions in the food industry
  • Food safety
Training objectives

Train professionals capable of:

  • Transform agricultural raw materials into simple or elaborate food products;
  • Define, implement and control the application of standards concerning quality, health, safety and the environment;
  • Set up new processes on an industrial scale, adapt manufacturing parameters and equipment until satisfactory production is achieved
